Section 2:

Frequently Asked Questions

A few quick answers to help you understand how DYDD Live works and whether it’s the right fit for your group.

Who is Discover Your Divine Design Live for?
Discover Your Divine Design Live is designed for individuals, small groups, church teams, ministries, and organizations who want to grow in faith, gain clarity, and better understand how God has uniquely designed them to serve.
How long is the program and what formats are available?
DYDD Live is flexible and can be delivered in several formats, including a one-day intensive, a series of Saturday sessions, or a 4-, 6-, or 8-week evening experience. Each format includes live teaching, discussion, and guided application.
What do participants receive during the experience?
Participants receive live teaching, visual presentation content, guided discussion, and practical tools for reflection and application. Depending on the format, this may also include the book, workbook, assessments, and supporting materials.
Can we host this at our church or organization?
Yes. DYDD Live is designed to be hosted by churches, ministries, and organizations. We work with you to shape the schedule, format, and experience so it fits your people, your setting, and your goals.
What topics are covered in the DYDD Live sessions?
The journey covers six core areas: Identity, Expertise, Story, Desire, Spiritual Gifts, and Niche. Together, these themes help participants see how God has been shaping their life and calling.
What should participants do to prepare?
Preparation depends on the format of your event. In some cases, participants may be invited to complete an assessment or review selected materials in advance, but this is not always required. Any recommended preparation will be communicated clearly before the experience begins.
Is there a minimum or maximum group size?
DYDD Live can be adapted for a wide range of group sizes, from smaller gatherings to larger church or organizational events. We tailor the structure, discussion flow, and delivery to fit your audience and setting.
Can this be used as part of a discipleship or leadership development program?
Absolutely. Many churches and organizations use DYDD Live as a strong foundation for discipleship, leadership development, team growth, and ministry alignment.

What This Assessment Is—and Is Not

What This Assessment Is

This Spiritual Gifts Assessment is a reflective tool designed to support prayerful discernment. It helps you notice patterns of service, motivation, and spiritual expression that may point toward how the Holy Spirit is working in and through your life.

It is grounded in Scripture and shaped by the understanding that spiritual gifts are given by God for the good of others. The results are meant to encourage awareness, humility, and faithful participation in the Body of Christ.

What This Assessment Is Not

This assessment is not a personality test, a measure of spiritual maturity, or a declaration of identity. It does not place limits on what God can do through you, nor does it replace the role of Scripture, community, or the ongoing work of the Holy Spirit.

Spiritual gifts are best understood as they are lived out through love, service, obedience, and time. This assessment offers insight, not certainty; guidance, not labels.

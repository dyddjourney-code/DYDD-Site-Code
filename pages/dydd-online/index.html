What Is in the Online Course?

The Discover Your Divine Design online course is a transformational journey designed to help participants uncover their God-given identity, gifts, and purpose in a flexible but deeply engaging format.

Each module builds on the next, walking step-by-step through the six foundational areas of DYDD: Identity, Expertise, Story, Desire, Spiritual Gifts, and Niche.

Through video teaching, Scripture-rich reflection, journaling prompts, visual content, interactive elements, downloadable resources, and exclusive C.A.R.E.-based exercises, participants are invited not only to learn, but to begin living from the truth of how God has designed them.

Whether someone is navigating a season of transition, seeking greater clarity in how to serve, or longing to understand how their design fits within God’s larger story, DYDD Online is built to support that journey with depth and structure.

Section 2:

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few common questions about the Discover Your Divine Design Online experience.

How does DYDD Online work?
Discover Your Divine Design Online is a self-paced course accessible from anywhere. Participants move through video lessons, written reflections, interactive exercises, and downloadable resources designed to guide them through the full DYDD journey.

Each module builds on the previous one, helping you explore Identity, Expertise, Story, Desire, Spiritual Gifts, and Niche in a thoughtful and practical way.
Do I have to take the course at a specific time?
No. DYDD Online is designed to be flexible and self-paced. You can begin when you are ready and move through the material at a pace that works best for you.

Some participants move through the content quickly, while others prefer to take more time to reflect, pray, and process each section.
Can I take the course with others?
Yes. While many people take the course individually, DYDD Online also works well in a group setting such as a small group, ministry team, or church class.

Discussion prompts and reflection exercises make it easy for groups to process what they are learning together.
Do I need the book or workbook?
The online course is designed to work alongside the Discover Your Divine Design book and workbook, which provide deeper reflection and guided exercises.

It is strongly recommended that each participant have a personal copy of both the book and the workbook while taking the course.
Are the DesignID™ and Spiritual Gifts assessments required?
No. The course can be completed without them.

However, participants are encouraged to take the DesignID™ Assessment and the Spiritual Gifts Assessment as they provide helpful insight that complements the learning experience.
What will I have when I finish?
By the end of the course, participants will have a clearer understanding of how their identity, experiences, passions, and gifts fit together.

Many participants also complete a personal Ministry Declaration, helping articulate how they sense God calling them to contribute and serve.

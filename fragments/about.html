Who We Are

A Journey Rooted in Purpose

Discover Your Divine Design was created by John Willoughby, a leader, coach, and devoted follower of Christ who believes every person has been created by God with purpose, on purpose. Through years of serving leaders, churches, and teams, John recognized a common need: many believers longed for greater clarity about who they are and how to faithfully live out their calling.

That desire gave rise to Discover Your Divine Design. Drawing from experience in leadership development, biblical teaching, and personal growth, John built a process that is rooted in Scripture and shaped for real life. More than a book or program, DYDD is a guided journey that helps people explore their identity, expertise, story, desires, spiritual gifts, and niche within the Body of Christ.

At its heart, Discover Your Divine Design exists to help people grow in clarity, confidence, and courage as they live out the life God has entrusted to them — in the church, at home, and in the world.

On Purpose — For Purpose

Discover Your Divine Design is grounded in the truth of Ephesians 2:10 — that every believer has been intentionally created by God with purpose.

Mission

On Purpose

Discover Your Divine Design exists to help people rediscover who they are in Christ. Scripture tells us that we are God’s workmanship—His masterpiece—created intentionally and uniquely by Him.

Through biblical teaching, reflection, and practical tools, DYDD helps people gain clarity about their identity, story, gifts, and design so they can live with confidence in who God created them to be.

“For we are God’s handiwork, created in Christ Jesus…” — Ephesians 2:10
Vision

For Purpose

We envision a Church where believers understand their design and joyfully participate in the work God has prepared for them.

When people discover their identity and embrace their unique contribution, they find belonging, meaningful service, and a deeper sense of calling within the Body of Christ.

“…to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do.” — Ephesians 2:10

Frequently Asked Questions

A few helpful answers about what we believe, how DYDD works, and the heart behind this journey.

What is our statement of faith?
We believe the Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God and the ultimate guide for faith and life (2 Timothy 3:16–17). We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit (Matthew 28:19).

We believe salvation is a gift of God’s grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone—who lived a sinless life, died for our sins, and rose again to offer eternal life to all who believe (John 3:16; Ephesians 2:8–9).

We believe the Holy Spirit indwells every believer, equipping and empowering them to live a godly life and serve in the advancement of God’s Kingdom (Acts 1:8; 1 Corinthians 12:4–7).

We believe the Church is the Body of Christ, called to unity, love, and service, and that every believer is gifted by God to build up the Body and glorify Him (Romans 12:4–6; Ephesians 4:11–13).

We believe Jesus Christ will return to fulfill all of God’s promises, bringing final restoration and eternal life with Him to all who belong to Him (Revelation 21:1–5).
What do we believe about the Bible?
We believe the Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God, fully trustworthy and the final authority in all matters of faith and life (2 Timothy 3:16–17). It is the foundation for everything we teach and the standard that guides our process.
What makes our approach unique?
We integrate biblical truth with practical tools that help people see how their identity, expertise, story, desires, and spiritual gifts fit together.

DesignID serves as a reflective lens that helps reveal both natural strengths and areas for growth. When these elements are understood together, people gain clarity about how they can faithfully contribute within the Body of Christ.
How do you help people discover their design?
Through guided reflection, assessments, and biblical teaching, we walk people through six key areas: Identity, Expertise, Story, Desire, Spiritual Gifts, and Niche.

Each area reveals part of how God has been shaping your life. When they come together, people begin to see more clearly where they can serve and contribute within the Body of Christ.
What if I’m not sure where I fit in the Church?
That uncertainty is more common than people realize, and it is exactly why this journey exists.

Discover Your Divine Design helps people see how God has uniquely shaped them so they can step into meaningful participation within the Body of Christ with confidence and clarity.
Is Discover Your Divine Design affiliated with a specific denomination?
Discover Your Divine Design is rooted in historic Christian faith and grounded in Scripture. While it is not tied to one specific denomination, it is designed to serve believers, churches, and ministries that affirm the authority of the Bible and the centrality of Jesus Christ.

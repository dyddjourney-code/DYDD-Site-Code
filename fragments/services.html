Ways to Engage the DYDD Journey

In Person

DYDD Live

Discover Your Divine Design Live is an interactive, in-person experience designed to help participants uncover how God uniquely created them for purpose and impact.

Through live teaching, group discussion, and real-time application, participants engage their Identity, Expertise, Story, Desire, Spiritual Gifts, and Niche in a supportive, faith-filled environment.

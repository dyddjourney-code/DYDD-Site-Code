The DYDD Journey

Discover Your Divine Design equips believers to understand how God has uniquely shaped their life, gifts, and passions for meaningful service. This journey walks through six core areas that bring clarity and confidence so you can live On Purpose, For Purpose.

Identity · Expertise · Story · Desire · Spiritual Gifts · Niche

Six Steps. One Cohesive Journey.

Each step builds on the last—helping you connect what God has been forming in you, and where He may be calling you next.

Step 1

Identity

It all begins with knowing whose you are.

  • Maybe you’ve carried false labels.
  • Maybe you’ve struggled to see your worth.
  • Or maybe you’re ready to believe what God says about you.
Step 2

Expertise

It all builds with what you’ve learned.

  • Maybe your skills feel unimportant.
  • Maybe your strengths have gone unnoticed.
  • Or maybe you’re realizing how God has shaped you.
Step 3

Story

It all comes together through your story.

  • Maybe your past feels broken.
  • Maybe your experiences have hidden meaning.
  • Or maybe God’s hand is becoming clear.
Step 4

Desire

It all deepens with what stirs your heart.

  • Maybe there’s a longing you’ve ignored.
  • Maybe you’ve questioned your passions.
  • Or maybe God is stirring new purpose within you.
Step 5

Spiritual Gifts

It all strengthens when God equips you.

  • Maybe you’ve sensed God empowering you.
  • Maybe you’re still discovering your spiritual strengths.
  • Or maybe you’re stepping boldly into new areas.
Step 6

Niche

It all leads to where your design meets God’s purpose.

  • Maybe you’ve wondered how it all fits.
  • Maybe you’re ready to step into your calling.
  • Or maybe you’re still piecing it together.

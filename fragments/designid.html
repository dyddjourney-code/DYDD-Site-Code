Introducing DesignID

A lens for identity, grace, and contribution

DesignID™ begins with a biblical conviction: every person is made in the image of God and created to reflect something of His nature in the world. Scripture shows us that God works uniquely through His people, and DesignID helps bring language to that design.

At the center is God Himself — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Flowing outward are four divine reflections: Architect, Artisan, Shepherd, and Steward. These reflections help reveal how grace is expressed through your life and how you may naturally contribute within God’s Kingdom.

DesignID does not replace spiritual formation or biblical truth. It supports them by helping you see your design more clearly, name it more confidently, and steward it more faithfully.

The Four Reflections

Four ways God’s design may be reflected through you

These are not labels to limit you. They are lenses that help you better understand how grace, personality, and contribution may be expressed in your life.

Architect

Reflects vision, initiative, and a drive to create, lead, and move things forward with purpose.

Artisan

Reflects clarity, craftsmanship, and a desire to shape, refine, and express truth with beauty and precision.

Shepherd

Reflects care, empathy, and a heart to nurture, protect, and strengthen others through meaningful connection.

Steward

Reflects order, responsibility, and a commitment to sustain, organize, and faithfully carry what matters.

Integrative Reflections

Where reflections work together

Most people reflect more than one dimension strongly. Integrative Reflections reveal how two reflections combine to shape how you lead, build, relate, and contribute.

Visionary

Architect + Artisan

Innovation joined with wisdom — building what is both new and true.

Interpreter

Artisan + Shepherd

Compassionate communication that brings understanding and healing.

Guardian

Shepherd + Steward

Relational stability and protection that preserves what matters.

Overseer

Steward + Architect

Responsible creation that guards purpose with diligence.

Innovator

Architect + Shepherd

Empathetic initiative that moves people and ideas forward.

Craftsman

Artisan + Steward

Enduring excellence that refines and sustains what lasts.

DesignID helps bring clarity to every part of the process

  • Identity — anchors you in who you are in Christ and helps you see how grace is expressed through your design.
  • Expertise — highlights the strengths, patterns, and ways God has shaped your contribution over time.
  • Story — illuminates how your lived experience has formed perspective, compassion, and conviction.
  • Desire — connects what stirs your heart with how God wired you to move, serve, and build.
  • Gifts — reveals how spiritual gifts may operate through your design in distinct and intentional ways.
  • Niche — helps clarify where your life, gifts, experience, and energy converge in focused Kingdom purpose.

DesignID transforms the DYDD journey from reflection into revelation, helping you move from simply wondering about your design to seeing it clearly and stewarding it with confidence.

WHAT IS DYDD?

Discover how God designed you to live. On Purpose — For Purpose.

Rooted in Ephesians 2:10, Discover Your Divine Design is a guided journey of Scripture and reflection that helps you recognize who you already are, by design.

Ephesians 2:10
“For we are God’s handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do.”
The journey explores five core elements of your design:
  • Identity — Whose you are
  • Story — What shaped you
  • Expertise — What you’ve cultivated
  • Desire — What stirs your heart
  • Spiritual Gifts — How the Spirit empowers you

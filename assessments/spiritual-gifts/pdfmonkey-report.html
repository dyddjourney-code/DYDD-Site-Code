What is Discover Your Divine Design

On Purpose - For Purpose

DYDD is a journey that helps you recognize how God has uniquely shaped your life for His purpose. By exploring your identity, expertise, story, desires, gifts, and niche, you begin to see the intentional way God has designed you to love, serve, and make a difference in the Body of Christ and beyond.

At the heart of this journey is the belief that you were created On Purpose - For Purpose. Your life is not random, and your design is not accidental. God has formed you with intention, redeemed your story through Christ, and prepared good works for you to walk in faithfully.

Discovering Your Divine Design is not about striving, performing, or trying to impress God. It is about responding to His grace and living more fully as the person He created you to be. As you understand your DESIGN more clearly, you can serve with greater confidence, humility, and joy.

“For we are God’s workmanship, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do.” (Ephesians 2:10)

What were the DesignID reflection correlations
